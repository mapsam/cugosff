My Scenerio

I'm working for an online newspaper and want to connect some data to a map within the article. The article deals with over 200 dams in the US and their flowage.

Since a basic image won't suffice for the data I have so I want to make the map interactive as to allow the reader to find more information. Let's make it in d3!
